What is climate change?

The greenhouse gases, carbon dioxide- , methane- and nitrous oxide-levels have increased significantly in the last 150 years [1]. The greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere which raises the average temperature on the planet. This affects the ocean-currents and air-flow all around the world, which will directly influence the climate.
