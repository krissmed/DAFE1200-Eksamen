FAQ

Here we have some frequently asked questions

Which countries is this app available in?

This app is avilable inn all countries with a modern electric infrastructur.

What data do you collect?

We only collect nessecary data for the app to work as intended. This includes some basic personal information like name, date of birth, country of resident and e-mail. We also collect information about your power consumption . You can deactivate this at any time in settings in the app.

Is there any subscription fee?

The app is free to use. Therefor, is it not any fee or ads on the app.

Is it possible for the whole family to see the app?

Yes, there are multiple options. Option one is to have the graphs displayed through a Google Home. Option 2 is that you could share your login info with the people in your household.

Is there a desktop version?

The desktop version is currently in development and will be available in the near furture.
