The problem

As the world gets a higher population, the global power consumption will increase. This is a huge problem for the climate as much of the world power sources are non-renewable, as well as being harmful to the climate. Today, the global population is predicted to rise to 11 billion[1] people and the global wealth is set to increase. Both of these things point to a future with extremely high energy demands if we go by today's number. This is an issue of poverty, population growth and climate gas emissions.
