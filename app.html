Your power usage, organised in one easy-to-use app!

Front page

When you open your app, you will be greeted by a summary of your energy consumption room by room. With a quick overview you know exactly which room uses the most power. When you click on that room you can look at further details.

Rooms

You can also get a detailed overview of your power consumption room by room. The app will automatically detect the type of appliance connected to the socket and find the room. Here you can see the energy consumption compared to last week and the use of each appliance. The app will automatically detect what uses the most power and give you automated features to lower your electricity waste. You can find several automations that can be easily integrated in the automation part of the app.

Community-made automation

Here you can choose from a number of pre-programmed automations that can be integrated into your home in one keystroke. All you have to do is agree that Energy++ can access your various smart homes appliances, and the app will automatically give you suggestions for lowering your power consumption. These are made by the Energy++ community and by manufacturers. Each automation has a title, description and assessment. You can also filter and sort to find the automation that suits you best.

Easily switch electricity provider

Energy++ also gives you the opportunity to easily choose a climate-friendly energy supplier. Based on our algorithm that takes into the account the total greenhouse gas emissions, destroyed forestry, carbon capture and storage. We rank all electricity suppliers on a scale from sustainable (green globe), neutral (yellow) globe) and unsustainable (red globe). This gives you an indication of how sustainable each power supplier is. We also understand that sustainability is not everything when it comes to power supply. Therefore, we have created a pointsystem based on our user-experience with the suppliers and can use it, in addition to our durability index to make a good decision when it comes to your power supply.

Energy++ points

We at Energy ++ also reward people who make sustainable decisions. That is why we have our own pointsystem which gives you suitable gifts such as gift-cards for spas, cinemas, online stores etc. These points are earned through using the app. The less power and the more durable the power, the more points you earn. If you additionally help other Energy++ users, by giving a rating, create automation or expanding your community by recruiting your friend, you will earn even more points.
