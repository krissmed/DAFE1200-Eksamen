PLACEHOLDER

Your power usage, organised in one easy-to-use app!

Front page

The front page shows you a summary of how much energy you’ve been using this day, week, month and year. As you click on the icon you’ll get a more detailed report in the form of a daily/weekly/yearly report.

Points

Another page on the app is the points. When using the app and saving power by doing different things, you’ll earn points. You could also sign up to be a part of the Energy++ community where you can see your energy usage compared to your friends and family, as well as sharing different tips from the community page with them directly in the app. With these points, you can buy everything from clothing to gift cards from Energy++ partners.

Smart home Integration

The final page is a community driven tips site, where users and manufacturers easily can integrate one product with the rest of your smart home for different things, room, or power consumption sources based on your energy-history. This page also shows you how much power you can save by doing the different tips. This can be integrated with different products and services like google home, or amazon alexa to make it easier to control. Here people can create and publish solutions like “goodnight google/alexa” will turn off the lights in rooms which are not in use during the night. You can also integrate it with other smart products like robotvacum, smart fridge, smart tv, etc. for the community to make different solutions to everyday problems. Another simple integration would be to turn off the lights when you can see that you’re not home by using phone location.
