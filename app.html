Your power usage, organised in one easy-to-use app!

Front page

When you open your app, you will be greeted by a summary of your energy consumption room by room. With this you will get a quick and easy overview of what room is consuming hte most amount of energy. You can click on a room to view further details.

Rooms

We in Energy++ would like to give you a detailed overview of your energy consumption as possible. Therefore, we've made it possible to view detailed stats of every room in your house. Here you can see the energy consumption compared to last and see exactly what is drawing the most power. There is no tedious setup process. You just plug your Energy++ module in your fusebox and you're set. The app will automatically detect what is plugged into the socket and collect data about its power usage.

Community-made automation

As well as showing your power consumption, the app also makes it easier for you to save power by using Community made automations. Here you can integrate automatic actions into your smart home, such as turning off the light when you leave the house. All you do is press "integrate", and the app will ask for authorization from your different smart home appliances. Also, if you come up with an idea of your own, you can easily make it yourself and publish it for the community to use it want. It's that easy!

Easily switch electricity provider

We in Energy++ know how difficult it is to find reliable AND green energy supplier. Therefore, we have made it super easy to compare Energy++ also gives you the opportunity to easily choose a climate-friendly energy supplier. Based on our algorithm that takes into the account the total greenhouse gas emissions, destroyed forestry, carbon capture and storage. We rank all electricity suppliers on a scale from sustainable (green globe), neutral (yellow) globe) and unsustainable (red globe). This gives you an indication of how sustainable each power supplier is. We also understand that sustainability is not everything when it comes to power supply. Therefore, we have created a pointsystem based on our user-experience with the suppliers and can use it, in addition to our durability index to make a good decision when it comes to your power supply.

Energy++ points

We at Energy ++ also reward people who make sustainable decisions. That is why we have our own pointsystem which gives you suitable gifts such as gift-cards for spas, cinemas, online stores etc. These points are earned through using the app. The less power and the more durable the power, the more points you earn. If you additionally help other Energy++ users, by giving a rating, create automation or expanding your community by recruiting your friend, you will earn even more points.
