Your power usage, organised in one easy-to-use app!

Front page

As you open your app, you are greeted by a summary of your energy usage in a room-by-room basis. At a quick glance you know exactly what room is using the most electricity. When you click on that room you can look into further details.

Rooms

You can also get a detailed view of your power electricity usage on a room by room basis. The app will automatically detect what type applience is connected to the socket and assume which room it is. Here you can see the energy usage compared to last week and the usage of each applience. The app will automatically detect what is using the most electricity and give you community made automations for lowering your electricity waste. You can find more automation that can easily be integrated in the automation section of the app.

Community made automation

Here you can choose from a range of pre-programmed automation that can be integrated into your home with one click. All you need to do is authorize that Energy++ can access your different smart home appliences, and the app will automatically give you suggestion for lowering your power consumption. These are made by the Energy++ community and by manufacturers. Each automation has a title, description and rating. You can also filter and sort to find the automation that suits you the best.

Easily switch electricity provider

Energy++ also give you the opportunity to easily choose susatainable energy provider. Based on our algorithm which takes into account total climate gas emisions, destroyed forestry, carbon capture and storage, etc. we rate all electricity provider on a scale of sustainable (green globe), neutral (yellow globe) and non-sustainable (red globe). This gives you an easy-to-understand indication of how sustainable each electricity provider is. We also understand that sustainablility is not everything when it comes to electricity provider. Therefore we've made a rating system based on our user experience with the provider and can use that, in addition to our sustainablility index to make a good decision when it comes to your electricity provider.

Energy++ points

We in Energy++ also reward people who take sustainable decisions. Therefore we also have a point system which gives you befits such as giftcard to spas, cinemas, online stores, etc. These points are earned through using the app, but the less electricity and the more susatainable your electricity is, the more points you get. In addition to that, if you're helping out other Energy++ user, by leaving a rating, making automations or growing the community by refering your friend, you will earn even more points.
